Data principles

  • Primary sources only — no aggregators, no scrapers
  • Every number has a source citation on the chart
  • Limitations and measurement gaps explicitly noted
  • Scientific consensus stated clearly (climate, health)
  • Predictions show algorithm and confidence interval
  • No commercial interests, no advertising
